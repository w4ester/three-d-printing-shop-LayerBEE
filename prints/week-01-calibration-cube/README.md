# Week 1: Calibration Cube

Your first print! A calibration cube helps you verify your printer is working correctly.

## What You'll Need
- PLA filament (any color)
- Your 3D printer leveled and ready

## Recommended Settings
| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Layer Height | 0.2mm |
| Infill | 20% |
| Nozzle Temp | 200C |
| Bed Temp | 60C |
| Print Speed | 50mm/s |

## Download
- [Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1278865) - Original XYZ Calibration Cube

## Success Criteria
- Each side measures 20mm (+/- 0.2mm)
- Letters X, Y, Z are clear and readable
- No warping or layer separation
- Clean first layer

## Share Your Print!
1. Take a photo of your cube
2. Measure each dimension
3. Fork this repo and add to `community-prints/`

## Troubleshooting
- **Dimensions off?** Check belt tension and steps/mm calibration
- **Warping?** Increase bed temp or use adhesive
- **Layer lines?** Check Z-axis and belts

Happy printing! - LayerBEE
